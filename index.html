About Us

At Staged 4 Sale, we don’t just stage homes — we curate elevated living experiences that captivate buyers and command top offers. Led by Tawni Oppenheim, one of the nation’s few professionals to hold the prestigious Accredited Staging Professional Master™ (ASPM™) designation, our team brings over years of expertise in design, marketing, and emotional appeal. A pioneer of home staging, Tawni was personally trained by Barb Schwarz, the creator of the industry itself. She blends artistic vision with real estate insight to position every property at its fullest potential.

From occupied homes to vacant listings, we approach each project with careful intention and an eye for detail. Our signature services—such as TruREdesign©, which refreshes spaces using existing furnishings, and Staged4Home©, which reimagines lived-in spaces for homeowners—reflect our commitment to both beauty and practicality. With a reputation for professionalism, creativity, and results, Staged 4 Sale helps sellers move forward with confidence and style.

Staging:

It turns hesitation into action — and houses into homes buyers can't forget.

During your consultation, we'll help determine what's best for your property — but most of the time, we recommend vacant staging. Why?

Because while we love your personal style, home staging relies on a more neutral, broadly appealing look that allows buyers to project their own lives into the space. Vacant staging gives us the most creative and strategic control to do that effectively.

That said, if you're living in the home or working with a limited timeline or budget, we also offer:

  • Occupied staging using your existing pieces (with our TruREdesign™ approach)
  • Re-Design+, which blends your furnishings with key accessories and furniture from our inventory

No matter the approach, we'll work with you to make sure your home looks its best.
