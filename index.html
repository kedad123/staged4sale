About Us

At Staged 4 Sale, we don’t just stage homes — we curate elevated living experiences that captivate buyers and command top offers. Led by Tawni Oppenheim, one of the nation’s few professionals to hold the prestigious Accredited Staging Professional Master™ (ASPM™) designation, our team brings over years of expertise in design, marketing, and emotional appeal. Personally trained by Barb Schwarz, the creator of home staging, Tawni blends artistic vision with real estate insight to position every property at its fullest potential.

From occupied homes to vacant listings, we approach each project with careful intention and an eye for detail. Our signature services—such as TruREdesign©, which refreshes spaces using existing furnishings, and Staged4Home©, which reimagines lived-in spaces for homeowners—reflect our commitment to both beauty and practicality. With a reputation for professionalism, creativity, and results, Staged 4 Sale helps sellers move forward with confidence and style.

Our Process

1. Consultation & Assessment

We begin with a thorough walk-through of your property, identifying its strengths and areas for improvement.

2. Strategic Planning

Based on the assessment, we develop a customized staging plan tailored to your home's unique features and target market.

3. Staging & Transformation

Our team meticulously stages your home, arranging furniture, decor, and accessories to create an inviting atmosphere.

4. Showcase & Sell

Your property is now ready to impress potential buyers, leading to faster sales and maximum value.
